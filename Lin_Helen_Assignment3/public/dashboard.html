Best Boba November 29, 2022, 9:12 PM

First, I thought that this was one of those cutesy looking drink places and just questioned if it was actually good. Well, it was super good. I had one of the Cloud Drinks, Sunny Day (Blue Citrus). It reminded me of my childhood when my cousins and aunts would put rainbow sherbet in the punch or vanilla ice cream in the punch with less sweetness. I thought it was super cool how the cream does look like clouds. The other people in my party also ordered Cloud Drinks; Pinky Bunny and Sulley's Cloud. There is also a cute background mural to take photos. Again, this is another spot where the drinks are as good as the photos.
